Pennsylvania does not report race/ethnicity data for tests or hospitalizations.

Notes

  • As of March 7, 2021, Pennsylvania has reported race data for 63% of cases and 93% of deaths, and ethnicity for 33% of cases and 94% of deaths. Pennsylvania does not report either race or ethnicity data for tests or hospitalizations.
  • Our calculations assume Hispanic or Latino ethnicity may include any race. Race categories are mutually exclusive and include both Hispanic/Latino and non-Hispanic/Latino ethnicity.
  • Population sizes are taken from the Census Bureaus 2019 ACS 5-Year estimates Table B02001 for race and B03002 for ethnicity.
  • One or more standard race categories are not reported or otherwise specified by the state. These categories may or may not be included in the "Other race" category in our data.
